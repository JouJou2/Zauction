Shipping & Pickup

Delivery options and buyer responsibilities

Buyer Responsibility

Buyers are responsible for arranging and paying for all shipping, packing, and insurance. We do not provide in-house shipping services but can recommend professional art shippers and packing specialists.

Items must be collected or shipping arranged within 14 days of payment clearance. Storage fees may apply for items left beyond this period.

Recommended Shippers

We maintain a list of professional art shippers, packers, and freight forwarders who are experienced in handling antiques and fine art. Contact us for recommendations based on your location and the nature of your purchase.

Buyers are responsible for obtaining quotes, coordinating pickup, and paying shippers directly. We are not responsible for shipper performance, delays, or damage during transit.

Packing Standards

Professional packing is strongly recommended for fragile, valuable, or large items. Proper packing materials and techniques reduce the risk of damage during transit.

If you choose to pack items yourself, you assume all risk of damage. We can provide basic packing materials (boxes, bubble wrap, paper) for a fee, but we do not pack items.

Local Pickup

Local buyers may arrange to collect items in person. Pickup must be scheduled in advance by contacting our support team. We cannot accommodate walk-in pickups.

Pickup hours are typically Monday through Friday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. Appointments must be confirmed at least 48 hours in advance.

Buyers are responsible for bringing appropriate vehicles and assistance for loading. We do not provide loading services for large or heavy items.

International Shipping

International buyers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, import fees, and compliance with their country's import regulations.

We can provide export documentation (commercial invoices, certificates of origin) as needed. Buyers must arrange for customs clearance and payment of duties in their country.

Certain items may be subject to export restrictions or require permits. Buyers are responsible for verifying that items can be legally imported into their country before bidding.

Insurance

Buyers are responsible for insuring items during transit. Most professional shippers offer insurance as part of their service. Verify coverage limits and exclusions before shipping.

Risk of loss transfers to the buyer upon payment clearance. We are not responsible for damage or loss occurring after items leave our facility.

Storage Fees

Items must be collected or shipping arranged within 14 days of payment. After this period, storage fees of $10 per day (or 1% of the purchase price per week, whichever is greater) will apply.

Extended storage may be arranged by prior agreement. Contact us if you require additional time to arrange shipping.

Damage Claims

Inspect items carefully before accepting delivery. Note any damage on the shipping receipt and photograph the packaging and item immediately.

Damage claims must be filed with the shipper within the timeframe specified in their terms of service (typically 24-48 hours). We cannot assist with claims for damage occurring during transit.

Questions

For questions about shipping, pickup arrangements, or recommended shippers, contact our support team at support@zauction.com.

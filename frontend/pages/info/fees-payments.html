Fees & Payments

Understanding costs and payment methods

Buyer's Premium

A buyer's premium is added to the hammer price (your winning bid) of each lot. This fee covers administrative and operational costs and is standard practice in the auction industry.

The buyer's premium rate is stated in each auction's terms and conditions. Typical rates range from 20% to 25% of the hammer price.

Example: If you win a lot with a hammer price of $1,000 and the buyer's premium is 25%, your subtotal before shipping and taxes is $1,250.

Sales Tax

Sales tax may apply depending on the buyer's location and the nature of the item. Tax rates and applicability vary by jurisdiction.

Buyers are responsible for understanding their local tax obligations. We will collect and remit sales tax where required by law.

Payment Methods

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Bank Transfer / Wire Transfer: Preferred method for large purchases. No processing fees.
  • Credit Card: Visa, Mastercard, American Express accepted. A processing fee of 3% applies to credit card payments.
  • Certified Check / Bank Draft: Must clear before items are released. Allow 5-7 business days for clearance.

Personal checks are not accepted for first-time buyers or purchases over $5,000.

Payment Deadline

Payment is due within 7 days of the auction closing. Lots will not be released until payment is received in full and has cleared.

Late payments may incur a fee of 1.5% per month on the outstanding balance. Accounts with overdue balances may be restricted from bidding in future auctions.

Currency

All prices are listed in US Dollars (USD). Payments in other currencies will be converted at the prevailing exchange rate on the date of payment, plus any applicable bank fees.

Invoicing

Winning bidders receive an invoice via email within 24 hours of the auction closing. The invoice includes:

  • Hammer price (winning bid)
  • Buyer's premium
  • Applicable taxes
  • Payment instructions

Shipping costs are billed separately once arrangements are finalized.

Refunds and Returns

All sales are final. Returns are accepted only in cases of material misrepresentation as defined in our Terms of Service.

If a return is approved, refunds are processed within 14 days of receiving the returned item in its original condition. Buyer's premium and shipping costs are non-refundable.

Questions

For questions about invoices, payment methods, or fees, contact our support team at support@zauction.com.

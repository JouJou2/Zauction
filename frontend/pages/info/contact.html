For assistance with bidding, account access, payment questions, or shipping arrangements:

Email: support@zauction.com

Consignment Inquiries

If you are interested in consigning items for auction, please provide photographs and any available documentation (provenance, previous appraisals, receipts) to the appropriate specialist department.

Our specialists will review your submission and respond with an initial assessment. If your items are suitable for auction, we will discuss terms, timing, and reserve requirements.

Office Location

Zauction operates primarily online. Viewings of selected lots may be arranged by appointment. Please contact us to schedule a visit.

Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within two business days. During active auction periods, response times for bidding support may be faster. Complex consignment evaluations may require additional time for research and consultation.

Cookie Policy

How we use cookies and similar technologies

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, track your activity, and provide a better user experience.

Zauction uses cookies and similar technologies, including localStorage, to enhance functionality and analyze site usage.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the platform to function. They enable core features such as account login, bidding, and session management. Essential cookies cannot be disabled without affecting the platform's functionality.

Functional Cookies

These cookies remember your preferences and settings, such as language selection, display options, and watchlist items. They improve your experience by personalizing the platform to your preferences.

Analytics Cookies

These cookies collect information about how you use the platform, including pages visited, time spent, and interactions. This data helps us understand user behavior and improve our services. Analytics cookies do not identify you personally.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us monitor platform performance, identify technical issues, and optimize loading times. They collect data on page load speeds, errors, and browser compatibility.

localStorage

In addition to cookies, Zauction uses localStorage—a browser feature that stores data locally on your device. localStorage is used to maintain your account information, bidding history, watchlist, and preferences.

Data stored in localStorage remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers. Clearing your browser data or localStorage will delete your account information and bidding history from your device.

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (e.g., payment processors, analytics providers) that set their own cookies. These third parties have their own privacy policies and cookie practices.

We do not control third-party cookies and are not responsible for their use. Please review the privacy policies of third-party services for more information.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block cookies from specific websites
  • Block all cookies
  • Delete cookies when you close your browser

Please note that disabling cookies may limit certain features of the platform. Essential cookies are required for core functionality and cannot be disabled without affecting your ability to use the platform.

Browser-Specific Instructions

To manage cookies in your browser:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions → Manage and delete cookies

Do Not Track

Some browsers offer a "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal that requests websites not to track your activity. Currently, there is no industry standard for how to respond to DNT signals.

Zauction does not respond to DNT signals at this time. You can manage cookies and tracking through your browser settings as described above.

Updates to This Policy

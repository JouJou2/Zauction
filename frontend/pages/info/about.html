About Zauction

A trusted platform for curated timed auctions

Our Mission

Zauction connects discerning collectors with exceptional objects through carefully curated timed auctions. We specialize in antiques, fine art, rare books, ceramics, and collectibles of historical and aesthetic significance.

Our platform combines the accessibility of online bidding with the rigor and expertise of traditional auction houses. Every item offered has been evaluated by specialists and presented with detailed condition reports and provenance documentation.

Our Approach

We believe that quality surpasses quantity. Rather than overwhelming bidders with endless listings, we focus on thoughtful curation. Each auction is composed of items selected for their authenticity, condition, and cultural value.

Timed auctions allow participants from around the world to bid at their convenience, while maintaining the competitive dynamics and transparency of live sales. Bidding periods are clearly defined, increments are standardized, and all activity is recorded.

Trust and Transparency

Every lot is accompanied by comprehensive documentation including high-resolution photography, detailed descriptions, condition assessments, and available provenance. We disclose all known restorations, repairs, and imperfections.

Our buyer's premium and fee structure is clearly stated. Payment terms, shipping arrangements, and return policies are outlined before bidding begins. We operate with the same standards of accountability expected of established auction houses.

Expertise

Our team includes specialists in European decorative arts, Asian ceramics, rare books and manuscripts, fine art, and antique jewelry. Items are evaluated for authenticity, condition, and market value before acceptance into our auctions.

We work with independent experts, conservators, and appraisers when specialized knowledge is required. Authentication reports and third-party evaluations are obtained for items of significant value or uncertain attribution.

For Collectors

Whether you are building a collection, seeking a specific piece, or exploring a new area of interest, Zauction provides access to vetted objects with clear documentation. Our platform is designed for serious collectors who value expertise, transparency, and integrity.
